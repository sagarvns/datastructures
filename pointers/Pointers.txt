A Pointer is the address of a variable.

For example
int x=10,y=20;
int* p1=&x;
int* p2=&y;
variable name |  Variable Value | address
x               10                  1000
y               20                  1002
p1              1000                1004
p2              1002                1006

&x=1000
&y=1002
x=10
y=20
*p1=*1000=10 //Value at address 1000
*p2=*1002=20
